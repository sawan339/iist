WELCOME TO INDORE INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Indore Institute of Science &Technology (IIST) established in the year 2003, is amongst the Top 5 Engineering Colleges of Indore. The Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to RGPV Bhopal offering Bachelor’s degree in CS, IT, EC, Civil, Chemical and Mechanical Engineering and Master’s Degree in CS, Machine Design and Digital Communication. At IIST, we are committed to enhance the employability Quotient of the Students along with Holistic Development. Towards this we have dedicated Special Interest Groups (SIGs) on emerging areas like Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, Mechatronics, Additive Manufacturing, Renewable Energy, Sustainable Development, etc. These SIGs focus on the Capacity building of the faculty and students and enable them to take up the activities related to training, research and development on the emerging fields. IIST is also associated with IITs like IIT Bombay for e-yantra Robotics Lab, Spoken Tutorial and IIT Delhi for Virtual Lab and also have good Industry-Academia tie ups like the AWS Academy, RedHat Academy, Microsoft Imagine Academy, Facebook Developer Circles, Google Developer Student Clubs, MSME, etc. to bridge the gap between the requirements of the Industry and the curriculum through International/ National Certificate courses.

UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION

  • Interactive, Technology enabled ‘Flipped Learning’ Methodology through Various Associations like Virtual Lab IIT Delhi, NPTEL SWAYAM and e-Yantra Lab IIT Bombay etc.
  • Eminent Persons from MHRD NIRF ranked institutes as Principal & HoDs.
  • Specialization in Undergraduation through Special Interest Groups (SIGs) in association with AWS Academy, Microsoft Imagine Academy, Red Hat Academy, etc.
  • On Campus Internship (OCI) facility for students to get trained on latest technologies along with an internship project.
  • Cloud Based Training on Programming Languages using SkillRack Platform
  • Consultancy Based Industrial Visits (CIV) to enable student to identify the problems faced by the industry and propose innovative solutions.
  • Dedicated Career Development Centre (CDC) for Soft Skills & Language Training
  • Dedicated Research and Development Cell (RDC) with the state of the art infrastructure for conducting high quality research in emerging fields like IoT, iOS, AI, Robotics etc.
  • Centre for Application of Research in Engineering (CARE) to support Entrepreneurship and Incubation
  • Support for Participation in the National and International Coding Competitions, Hackathons, Technical Competitions etc.
